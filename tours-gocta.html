About the Gocta Waterfalls

The Gocta Waterfall is amongst the tallest of the world, and is a premier natural attraction of the Amazonas Region. It is over 771 meters tall, and is surrounded by minor falls which, combined, create an incredible natural pool at its base. Perfect for you to enjoy!.
